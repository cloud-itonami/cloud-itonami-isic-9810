# Governance

cloud-itonami-isic-9810 follows open-source governance with transparent
decision-making documented in ADRs.

Scope: Household own-account production-record logging, extension-worker/
enumerator survey-visit scheduling proposals, in-kind programme-support
coordination, and food-security-concern flagging for a structured
own-account-production tracking programme (see README.md's Scope section
for why ISIC 9810 — a national-accounts bookkeeping category, not a normal
business — is modeled this way).

Out-of-scope: Finalizing a welfare/aid-eligibility determination, directly
executing field/production work on a household's behalf, and suspending
or terminating a household's programme enrollment.

See README.md for complete CRITICAL scope exclusions.
